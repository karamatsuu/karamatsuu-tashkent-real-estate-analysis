# Tashkent Primary Real Estate Market Analysis (2026)

## Overview
This repository contains a data analysis project focused on the residential real estate market in Tashkent, Uzbekistan. The objective is to evaluate the pricing dynamics of new builds (primary market) across different urban districts and analyze the competitive positioning of major developers.

## Business Context
The Tashkent real estate market is experiencing a significant shift, with primary market demand severely outpacing the aging secondary stock. This analysis was conducted to:
1. Identify baseline pricing per square meter across distinct urban zones.
2. Highlight the "flight to quality" trend driving premiums in central districts.
3. Map the market positioning of specific developments in the Business and Comfort classes.

## Tech Stack
* **Language:** Python
* **Libraries:** Pandas (Data manipulation), Matplotlib (Data visualization)
* **Environment:** Jupyter Notebook

## Key Findings
* **Centralized Premium:** The Central and Shaykhontohur districts command the highest market premiums, driven by luxury developments.
* **Emerging Business Hubs:** Districts like Mirzo-Ulugbek represent a highly competitive "Business-class" cluster, offering strategic growth opportunities.
* **Pricing Strategy:** Analysis of unit sizes versus per-square-meter costs reveals distinct volume discounting strategies to stimulate family-oriented demand.

## Repository Contents
* `tashkent_residential_market_dataset_2026.csv`: The raw dataset compiled from local real estate aggregators and developer listings.
* `01_market_analysis_nrg_internship_task.ipynb`: The Jupyter Notebook containing data cleaning, feature engineering, and visualization code.
* `Tashkent_Real_Estate_Analysis.pdf`: A concise, 3-slide executive presentation summarizing the findings.
